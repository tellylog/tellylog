About tellylog:

Welcome to tellylog.com - a site for keeping track of your series and your viewing habits. Just sign up and start the fun!

Were does "tellylog" come from, you ask? Well, its a combiniation of the british slang for television "telly" and "log", for logging in the sense of keeping track of something.

What does tellylog do for you?
You can:
  • Check episodes of a series you have seen to add them to your Watchlog
  • get info on which episodes of a series you have already seen on your Watchlog
  • add the series you wish to see in the future to your Watchlist
